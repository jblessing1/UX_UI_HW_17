About Me

A UX/UI Design. For the past 5 years, I pursued Child Nursing as a career however, I've always longed to design full-time. At any given opportunity I have used my creative abilities to design t-shirts, and create personalised paintings for clients, make posters and flyers. I recently had the opportunity to increase my knowledge and understanding of design, specifically UX/UI.

I discovered UX/UI Design through a Bootcamp (Love Circular) and completed their course. Aside from completing the course I have worked with local churches to build and maintain their websites. In addition, I've used WordPress to build a blog called vulnerable feelings. When I'm not designing I am expressing myself creatively through music, reading, photography, and writing poetry.
